Terms and Conditions

Eligibility to Rent a Motorable Vehicle

To rent a motorable vehicle from us, you must be at least 18 years old and possess a valid driver's license. You must also meet any additional requirements specified in the rental agreement.

Documents

The following documents are required to rent a vehicle: a valid driver's license, a government-issued ID, and proof of address. Additional documents may be required based on the type of vehicle you are renting.

Things Included in Your Rental

Your rental includes the use of the vehicle, a full tank of fuel (unless otherwise specified), basic insurance coverage, and roadside assistance. Additional accessories or services may be available at an extra cost.

Fuel

Fuel is totally on you. You will get the vehicle with a full tank, and you have to return it with a full tank or take our vehicle with complimentary fuel to reach the nearest fuel station.

Additional Passengers

The number of passengers allowed in the vehicle is limited to the number of seats available. Additional charges may apply if the vehicle is used to transport more passengers than specified.

Vehicle Checkout

During vehicle checkout, you must inspect the vehicle for any pre-existing damage and report it to us. The vehicle condition will be documented, and any discrepancies will be noted in the rental agreement.

Driving Rules and Regulations

You must adhere to all local traffic laws and regulations while operating the vehicle. Any fines or penalties incurred during the rental period are the responsibility of the renter.

Misbehavior with Support Representatives

Any form of harassment or misbehavior towards our support representatives will not be tolerated. Such behavior may result in immediate termination of the rental agreement and additional charges.

Security

The security deposit is required to cover potential damages or losses. This deposit will be refunded after the vehicle is returned in satisfactory condition, minus any deductions for damages or extra charges.

Vehicle Return Policy

The vehicle must be returned on the agreed-upon date and time. Late returns may incur additional charges. If the vehicle is returned in a condition that requires excessive cleaning or repairs, additional fees will apply.

Extra Charges for Loss or Damage

Any loss or damage to the vehicle during the rental period will result in additional charges. The cost of repairs or replacement will be deducted from your security deposit or billed separately.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time will be eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the pickup time may incur a cancellation fee or no refund, depending on the terms specified in the rental agreement.
