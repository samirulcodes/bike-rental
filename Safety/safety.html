Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Please review the following guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable ride.

1. Wear a Helmet

Always wear a helmet while riding to protect yourself from potential head injuries.

2. Follow Traffic Rules

Adhere to all traffic laws, signals, and signs. Be mindful of pedestrians and other vehicles on the road.

3. Inspect the Bike Before Riding

Check the brakes, tires, and lights before starting your ride. Report any issues to us immediately.

4. Ride Within Your Limits

Do not attempt risky maneuvers or ride in conditions beyond your skill level.

5. Stay Visible

Wear bright or reflective clothing, especially when riding at night or in low-visibility conditions.

6. Keep Both Hands on the Handlebars

Always keep both hands on the handlebars, except when signaling a turn.
